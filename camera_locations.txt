OBSERVER quick camera guide

Load config command: "caster" (or "exec caster/caster")
            bind:    "DELETE"

For every map there are 5 predefined lerps (use with care):
- `v` - CT base
- `b` - T base
- `j` - Mid
- `n` - A site
- `m` - B site

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STATIC CAMERAS (press numpad button to jump to, hold arrow down to lerp to)

Layout on numpad:
| 7 | 8 | 9 |
| 4 | 5 | 6 |
| 1 | 2 | 3 |
|   0   | , |
