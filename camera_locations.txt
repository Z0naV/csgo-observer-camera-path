OBSERVER quick camera guide

Load config command: "caster" (or "exec caster/caster")
            bind:    "DELETE"

For every map there are 5 predefined lerps (use with care):
- `v` - CT base
- `b` - T base
- `j` - Mid
- `n` - A site
- `m` - B site
